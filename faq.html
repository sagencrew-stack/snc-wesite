Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for hiring teams and job seekers. Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at sagencrew@gmail.com.

How quickly can you share candidate profiles after I submit a requirement?
Our target is to acknowledge your requirement within 24 hours on business days. For most standard roles, the first batch of screened profiles arrives within 3–5 business days. Niche or leadership roles may take longer — we'll set clear expectations upfront.
How does your pricing model work?
We typically charge a percentage of the hired candidate's annual CTC, billed only when the candidate joins. For bulk or leadership engagements, we offer retainer-based or hybrid models. We'll share a transparent written quote after understanding your requirement — no obligation.
Do you offer replacement support if a candidate doesn't work out?
Yes. Standard engagements include a replacement guarantee window (typically 30–90 days depending on the role tier and package). The exact terms are included in your quote and agreement so there are no surprises.
Do you handle IT, non-IT, or only specific industries?
We support both. Our IT focus covers DevOps, Cloud, SRE, Software, Data, QA, and Infrastructure. Our Non-IT coverage includes HR, Sales, Finance, Operations, Customer Support, and Manufacturing. See the Industries section above for the full list.
How do you screen candidates before sharing profiles?
Every profile is human-screened — never auto-forwarded. We check role fit, skills alignment, experience relevance, location & notice period match, salary expectation alignment, and basic availability. You only see candidates who clear that filter.
Is the hiring process confidential?
Yes. For leadership or sensitive searches, we run mapped/confidential engagements where your company name isn't revealed to candidates until you approve. We can sign an NDA before sharing any details.
Can you handle bulk hiring or urgent drives?
Yes — bulk drives, walk-in supports, and team expansions are part of our Enterprise tier. Share your role count, timeline, and skill profile and we'll structure a dedicated delivery plan with weekly checkpoints.
How do I get started?
Submit your hiring requirement using the form below, email us at sagencrew@gmail.com, or message us on WhatsApp. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule a brief call and share a quote.
Do you charge candidates for resume or career services?
Yes — our resume writing, LinkedIn optimization, interview preparation, and career branding services are paid services. However, the free resume review and the five free tools above (ATS Score, CTC Calculator, JD Extractor, Cost of Hire, LinkedIn Headlines) are completely free with no obligation.
Can you guarantee me a job or interview calls?
No, and we want to be honest about that. No legitimate service can guarantee placement — anyone who does is misleading you. What we do is dramatically improve the quality of your resume, LinkedIn, and interview readiness so you become more visible to recruiters and more competitive when shortlisted.
How long does the resume / LinkedIn process take?
Typical turnaround is 5–7 days for the Fresher Pack, 7–10 days for the Professional Pack, and 10–14 days for the Executive tier. This includes drafting, your feedback, and revisions. Urgent timelines can be accommodated — talk to us.
How many revisions do I get?
Every package includes built-in revisions: 1 round for Fresher, 2 rounds for Professional, 3 rounds for Executive. We want you to be happy with the final result, so if reasonable adjustments are needed beyond the included rounds, we work it out — we don't nickel-and-dime.
What's the difference between an ATS-friendly resume and a regular one?
An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software employers use to scan resumes before a human ever sees them. ATS-friendly resumes use clean structure, standard section headers, role-relevant keywords, and avoid tables, columns, or graphics that confuse the parser. We make sure your resume passes ATS scans and still looks professional to humans.
Do you also help me apply for jobs?
We provide application support and strategy — which platforms to target, how to position yourself, how to use referrals, and how to follow up. We don't apply on your behalf in bulk, because that approach is low-quality and hurts your reputation. We focus on quality, targeted applications.
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. Your resume and details are never shared with employers without your explicit consent. We don't sell, distribute, or post candidate data anywhere. If you change your mind, we delete your records on request.
Are the free tools above really free?
Yes — no sign-up, no email required, no paywall. They run entirely in your browser, which means nothing you paste (resume text, JD content, salary numbers) is sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Use them as much as you like.
