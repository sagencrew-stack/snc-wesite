Team Purex

A fitness platform built for transformation, not just tracking.

Team Purex needed more than a website — they needed a platform that supports their brand, helps clients see real progress, and gives their trainers practical tools for workout and diet planning.

Fitness & Wellness Web Platform Brand Site Client Experience
Visit Live Site · teampurex.com
Client
Team Purex
Category
Fitness Platform
Scope
Brand + Web

What we built

  • Fitness brand website with a clean, motion-aware design
  • Client-focused digital experience that mirrors the in-gym brand
  • Transformation and progress tracking concept to show client journeys
  • Structured workout and diet planning framework for trainers
  • Business platform direction supporting both clients and trainers

Approach

We started with the brand. Fitness platforms often feel either too clinical or too aggressive — we aimed for something honest, premium, and motivating. From there we designed a client experience that respects how transformation actually happens (gradually, over months) and gives both clients and trainers practical tools rather than gimmicks.

Status

The platform is an active engagement and continues to evolve. We're not overclaiming finished features that aren't shipped yet — what's live serves real Team Purex clients today, and we're iterating with them on what comes next.
