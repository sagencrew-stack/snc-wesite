The Pipeline

Seven stages. One platform. Zero gaps.

Every stage of hiring usually lives in a different tool, spreadsheet, or someone's head. Sage Hire Stack connects them so nothing falls through the cracks.

01

Define

AI-assisted job descriptions. Skills auto-extracted. Budget estimated.

02

Source

Match against our sourced pool. Surface the top 10–20 in minutes.

03

Screen

Automated screening questions. Responses scored. Fit ranked.

04

Shortlist

Kanban view. Move candidates: Interview · Reject · Hold.

05

Interview

Calendar coordination. Auto feedback forms. Scorecards tracked.

06

Offer

CTC calculator. Compliant offer letter. Sent to candidate, tracked.

07

Onboard

Acceptance tracked. Joining paperwork generated. Status: HIRED.

Common questions

When will Sage Hire Stack launch? +

Beta with early-access waitlist members: Q3 2026. Public launch: Q4 2026. The exact rollout depends on feedback from the first 30 waitlist users.

What will it cost? +

Pricing is being finalized with input from waitlist members. Expected: a free tier for small teams (up to 3 active roles) and paid tiers scaling with role count. Early-access users get a permanent discount.

Does it replace recruiters? +

No. It makes recruiters and hiring managers more effective. The platform handles repetitive work (screening questions, scheduling, status tracking, offer paperwork) so humans can focus on the parts that matter — judgment, conversations, decisions.

Will it integrate with our existing ATS / HRIS? +

Yes — top priority. We'll launch with CSV import/export and standard webhook support so you can plug it into Greenhouse, Lever, Zoho Recruit, Keka, or your spreadsheet.

Where is the data stored? Is it secure? +

Data is stored in India-region cloud infrastructure. We follow DPDP Act 2023 guidelines for handling candidate PII. Detailed security documentation will be shared with early-access users before beta access.

Is this connected to Sage & Crew Next's recruitment services? +

Yes. Sage Hire Stack is built by the team that already delivers recruitment services to clients today. The platform is informed by hundreds of real placements and reflects how a working recruitment team actually operates.
