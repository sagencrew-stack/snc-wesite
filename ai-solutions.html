What we mean by "AI"

We use AI tools — large language models, document processors, automation platforms — to deliver client work faster and smarter. We don't claim every tool is AI. We don't claim end-to-end autonomous AI. We use AI where it actually moves the needle, and we mark everything else as future possibilities. That honesty is part of the service.

How we deliver AI work

No hype, no "AI everything." A simple, honest process.

1
Identify where AI actually helps

We start with your workflow, not a tool. Sometimes the answer is "this doesn't need AI — it needs automation" or "your team already does this well, leave it alone."

2
Pick boring, proven tools

Established LLMs, mature automation platforms, well-tested APIs. We avoid trendy AI tools that may not exist in 12 months and leave you stranded.

3
Keep humans in the loop

AI-generated drafts get human review. AI-screened candidates get human screening. AI insights get human interpretation. Speed up, don't replace.

4
Hand over so you're not locked in

Your AI workflows run in your accounts (OpenAI, Make, Zapier, whatever we pick) — not ours. You own the keys, the data, and the option to switch later.
